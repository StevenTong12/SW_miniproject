Who does COVID-19 affect?

It is apparent that many people who die from COVID-19 are majority elderly people or people with pre-existing conditions.

HOWEVER, that does not mean that COVID-19 will not affect those who are young and do not have pre-existing conditions!

There are so many young people who were healty and had no pre-existing conditions that suffered greatly from permanent lung damage and sometimes even sense of smell or taste. Do you really want to go through that?

Tips on how to prevent the spread!

What should I do if

I show symptoms or test positive?

1. Quarantine yourself! If you live with others, make sure you find a room away from others to quarantine yourself. The CDC recommends that people who do show symptoms or test positive should quarantine for 2 weeks.

2. If your symptoms are severe please have someone call 9-11 immediately!

3. DO NOT INTERACT with ANYONE! Even if you are asymptomatic or have mild symptoms it does not mean that other people's body will react the same way!
