Random Tip!

Washing your hands!

1. Always rinse first! This will make soaping up your hands easier and rinse away any dirt or grim on your hands.

2. Get some soap and lather your hands thoroughly by making sure you get the palms, back of your hands, between the fingers and under your nails.

3. Scrub your hands for at least 20 seconds and rinse off all of the soap. Remember, rinsing off all the soap will ensure bacteria will be rinsed off of your hands.
